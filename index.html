Bio

I grew up in the 80s with He-Man, G.I. Joe, Transformers, the Commodore 64 and stacks of comic books, all of which fueled my love for visual storytelling. I earned a Bachelor of Arts in Graphic Design from the University of Lapland and, after a few years in the field, moved into the game industry in 2008. I've stayed ever since because I love creating immersive experiences where visuals, sound and gameplay come together.

I'm comfortable working solo or with a team, and my background in both game art and graphic design gives me a versatile skill set for taking ideas from concept to final production.

This portfolio is just a tiny glimpse of what I can do. I work in both 2D and 3D, and I enjoy jumping between styles, whether it's adorable and bright or full-on horror.

If you think we might work well together, feel free to contact me at veera@nekobolt.net.
