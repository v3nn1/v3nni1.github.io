Bio

I grew up in the 80s with He-Man, G.I. Joe, Transformers, the Commodore 64 and stacks of comic books, all of which fueled my love for visual storytelling. I earned a Bachelor of Arts in Graphic Design from the University of Lapland and, after a few years in the design field, found my way into indie game development in 2008. I've stayed ever since because I love creating immersive experiences where visuals, sound and gameplay come together.

I'm comfortable working solo or with a team, and my background in both game art and graphic design gives me a flexible skill set from concept to final production. I draw from a wide visual library and enjoy working across styles—from cute to horror, and from stylized to realism.

If you think we might work well together, feel free to contact me at veera(at)nekobolt.net.
